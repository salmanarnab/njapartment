Thank you for showing interest in our programme. These are a few frequently asked questions to give you a clear idea of what's going on



  • 1. What is NanjingApartments? +
    • It is a branch of Connections China that serves the expat community in Nanjing helping them find apartments, sign contracts, communicate with landlords/ladies, move to a new house and handle cleaning and repairs in their apartments
  • 2. What will I have to do? +
    • You will be trained on how to handle various tasks. You will then be given on-field orientation and later on handle these different tasks on your own. Apart from these, you will also be involved in a few promotional drives.
  • 3. What are the main requirements? +
    • Communication skills in Chinese is important. Ability to speak other languages will be a plus/advantage.
  • 4. Will I get paid? +
    • Yes. Salaries will be based on clients introduced, contracts signed and tasks completed.
  • 5. Is it a full time job? +
    • This is not a full time job as we can only legally employ working visa holders on a full time basis.
  • 6. When can I start? +
    • You can begin immediately after we approve your application and allow you to join us.
  • 7. How can I apply? +
    • Please apply via the application linkAfter applying, we will email you back and possibly invite you to our office for an interview.
  • 8. Is there anything else I can do at the company? +
    • We're a very open minded group. Always feel free to email us or book and appointment at our office to discuss anything and everything. You're also free to raise any questions at the interview.
