Nanjing-Apartments is a branch of Connections China that serves the expat community in Nanjing helping them find apartments, sign contracts, communicate with landlords/ladies, move to a new house and handle cleaning and repairs in their apartments.

We have been helping out expats find comfortable and well priced apartments in Nanjing for over a year now. It has been massively successful and we'd like to thank all of you for all the support so far.
